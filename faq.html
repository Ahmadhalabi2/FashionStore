Frequently Asked Questions

Shopping & Orders

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) and overnight delivery options are also available.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days.

What are your shipping costs?

Free shipping on orders over $75. Standard shipping is $5.99, Express is $12.99. International rates vary by destination.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on unworn items with original tags. Items must be in original condition for a full refund.

How do I return an item?

Log into your account, go to order history, and select "Return Item". Print the prepaid return label and drop off at any authorized location.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes! Exchanges for different sizes or colors are free within 30 days. Use our online exchange portal for fastest processing.

Account & Technical

How do I create an account?

Click "Create Account" on the login page and fill in your details. You'll need a valid Gmail address to register.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a reset code to create a new password.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties.
