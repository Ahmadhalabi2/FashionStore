Payment FAQs

When will my payment be charged?

Your payment is charged immediately when you place your order.

Can I change my payment method after ordering?

Payment methods cannot be changed after order confirmation. Please contact customer service if you need assistance.

What if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, please check your card details and try again. Contact your bank if the issue persists.

Do you store my payment information?

We use secure tokenization and never store your full payment details on our servers.
